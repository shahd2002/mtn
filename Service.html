    Roaming Services


    Postpaid Roaming

    • Overview
    • Product list


    Stay Connected and Access all Countries of the World with International Roaming

     

    Take your postpaid line anywhere you travel and benefit from MTN’s international roaming service.
    Make and receive your calls and messages in all countries of the world.

     

    How to Subscribe:

    Visit one of MTN’s service centers with your personal identification card.

     

    Prices:

    • There is no monthly subscription.
    • Make a deposit of 60,000 SYP.
    • Cost of calls and messages while roaming are found as below list.
      *Cost is updated on daily basis.

     

    How to Use Service:

    You can directly use international roaming when traveling just by selecting a network in the host country.

     

    Additional Information:

    • You first have to subscribe to the international access service.
    • Country codes have to be used while roaming.

    Stay Connected and Use Your Prepaid Line Wherever You Go

     

    Take your prepaid line anywhere you travel and benefit from MTN’s prepaid roaming service. Make and receive your calls and messages overseas only in certain countries, according to roaming agreements between networks.

     

    How to Subscribe:

    Visit one of MTN’s service centers with your personal identification card, or call customer service at 111 for free.

     

    Prices:

    •   Cost of calls and messages while roaming are found in the following link:

     

    How to Use Service:

    •   You can directly use international roaming when traveling just by selecting a network in the host country. (only available in some countries).

    •   Dial *123*0096394400000# (only available in some countries according to host country’s network).

     

    Additional Information:

    •   You can transfer money to your line from Syria while roaming.

    •   To check the balance on your line while roaming, dial *100# for free.
